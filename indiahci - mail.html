Join Us at India HCI 2025

India HCI is the premier annual conference on Human-Computer Interaction in India, bringing together researchers, designers, industry professionals, and students to advance the field of HCI.

Organized in cooperation with the HCI Professionals Association of India (HCIPAI) and ACM SIGCHI, with all accepted proceedings archived in the ACM Digital Library.
